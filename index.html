Brand Identity

Kneuralabs LLC is an AI governance consulting firm empowering U.S. mid-market enterprises with structural alignment and regulatory compliance. We operate at the intersection of technology, policy, and business strategy.

Brand Positioning

Mission
To make AI governance accessible, precise, and commercially valuable for enterprise organisations worldwide.
Vision
A world where AI is deployed responsibly by default — compliance built in, not bolted on.
Promise
Safety is not a feature you add later. It is the foundation upon which intelligence is built.
Tagline
Structural AI Governance.
Audience
U.S. mid-market enterprises — CROs, CTOs, General Counsel, AI programme leads.
Standards
NIST AI RMF ISO/IEC 42001 EU AI Act SOC 2 UK AI Management Essentials

Voice and Tone

AttributeDefinitionExample
AuthoritativeWe state positions clearly and without hedging. Our expertise is our authority."Governance is not optional — it is the prerequisite for responsible deployment."
PreciseWe name the regulation, cite the standard, and define the term. No approximations."Your NIST AI RMF Tier 2 assessment indicates structural gaps in risk categorisation."
DirectActive voice. Short declarative sentences. No passive constructions."We identify the gap. We build the framework. You deploy with confidence."
ProfessionalFormal register. UK English. No contractions, colloquialisms, or filler phrases."The organisation must establish a governance function prior to deployment."
Non-alarmistWe acknowledge risk factually, not emotionally. No fear-based language."This system poses a Tier 3 risk under EU AI Act Article 10. Here is the remediation path."

Colour Rules

Filter by rule type
D OUse Electric Cyan (#00F0FF) as the sole accent colour for CTAs, rule lines, and active states.
D OUse Charcoal (#141414) for H1 headings and all primary display text.
D OUse Deep Navy (#204376) for H2, borders, and structural document elements.
D OKeep page backgrounds White. Smoke (#EEF1F5) for alternating rows only.
D OUse Logo Art colours (Teal, Crimson, Electric Blue) only in charts and diagrams.
D OEnsure WCAG AA contrast: 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large display text.
D O N TUse more than 3 colours on any single page or slide.
D O N TUse any blue, teal, or cyan not defined in this palette.
D O N TUse brand Crimson (#C5062A) to indicate errors — use Alert Red (#E53E3E) instead.
D O N TPlace Electric Cyan text on white backgrounds for body copy — insufficient contrast.
D O N TUse Semantic colours (Emerald, Alert Red, Amber) for decoration or branding.
W A R NCyan (#00F0FF) is accent only. It fails WCAG AA at small sizes on white. Always pair with dark text for legibility.

05

Document Application

All client-facing documents follow a defined structural hierarchy. Consistency across Word, PowerPoint, and Excel is mandatory.

Universal Document Rules

D OPlace the Art + Text Lockup logo in the document header at the approved size for the format.
D OUse Near Black (#0A141F) for all table headers across Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
D OUse Smoke (#EEF1F5) for alternating table rows and secondary backgrounds.
D OInclude version number and date in document footers using JetBrains Mono 9pt.
D ONumber all pages in footer: right-aligned, format "Page X of Y".
D O N TUse Word's built-in heading styles without overriding to match KNEURALABS type standards.
D O N TApply custom fonts beyond Inter and JetBrains Mono in any deliverable.
D O N TEmbed images as JPEG where PNG with transparency is available.
W A R NAlways flatten editable fields before sending final documents to clients. Never share live template files.

AI Master Block

The AI Master Block is the definitive prompt context for any AI model generating KNEURALABS-branded content. Paste verbatim at the start of every AI session.

Usage Protocol Paste the full block below at the beginning of any AI session producing KNEURALABS content. Do not paraphrase or summarise. All AI-generated output must be reviewed against this document before external distribution.
AI-MASTER-BLOCK — KNEURALABS BRAND CONTEXT 
COLOUR SYSTEM — 7 CORE COLOURS
  FROM PALETTE:
    Cyan:       #00F0FF — accent only: CTAs, rule lines, section labels
    Near Black: #0A141F — table headers, dark UI, deepest authority
    Deep Navy:  #204376 — H2, structural borders, secondary elements
    Charcoal:   #141414 — H1, display type, primary wordmark
  FROM LOGO ART:
    Steel Teal: #468DA3 — chart series 1, data visualisations
    Crimson:    #C5062A — chart series 3, risk callouts (not for errors)
    Electric Bl:#0A98D9 — chart series 2, technology diagrams
  SEMANTIC (status only):
    Emerald: #00B871 | Alert Red: #E53E3E | Amber: #F6A623
  RULES: Max 3 colours per page. No colours outside this palette.

LOGO — HARDCODED AI RULES
  Logo = Art Mark + Text Logo (KNEURALABS in Inter Bold White).
  File format: ALWAYS PNG with transparent background. NEVER JPEG.
  WHEN TO USE:
    Art + Text Lockup: all formal client-facing contexts (DEFAULT).
    Art Mark Only: icons, favicons, narrow headers, square formats.
    Text Logo Only: footer lines only, after art mark has appeared.
  SIZING (height only — width always auto):
    Word header: 22px | Word body: 80px | PPT title: 120px
    PPT slide: 40px  | HTML nav: 36px  | HTML mobile: 28px
    HTML hero: 96px  | Email: 48px     | Favicon: 32px
  Clear space = 25% of logo height on all four sides.
  PROHIBITED:
    No background rectangle behind the logo.
    No crop, rotate, flip, or visual effects on the art mark.
    No manual recreation of the art mark geometry.

FONTS
  Primary: Inter (fallback: Arial) | Mono: JetBrains Mono
  Word/Excel body: 10pt | PowerPoint body: 18pt minimum
  Bold: headings and key terms only. Underline: hyperlinks only.
  No italics. No decorative fonts.

REFUSE TO:
  - Use colours outside the defined 7-colour brand palette.
  - Use JPEG for logo files.
  - Set both width and height on the logo simultaneously.
  - Use italics anywhere in any output.
  - Use passive voice, hedging language, or casual tone.
  - Use Crimson (#C5062A) to indicate errors — use Alert Red instead.
  DEFAULT: White bg | Charcoal H1 | Navy H2 | Near Black body | Cyan accent.
